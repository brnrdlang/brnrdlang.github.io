Bernhard Lang

I am a scientific researcher and visual artist. I started in the sciences, but I am now exploring the opportunity for an alternative to a classic academic carrier.

I want to explore visual perception through an artistic lens. Furthermore I want to make scientific discoveries of the field accessible to everyone, through the use of digital media.

Over the years of my academic and scientific education, I aquired expertise in the following fields:

  • Data science: analysis and visualization
  • 3D graphics and illustrations
  • Cognitive science and visual perception

Next to my personal publicistic and artistic projects I offer consulting services for all of these topics. If you want to work with me, please contact me on one of the channels mentioned below.
